Becoming

The butterfly is all about the waiting.
x
And all that happens to it happens
not by force or under its own power.

It knows only existence as a furry length

fingering around for plants in a higher light,
the wrap of damp transformational cloth

and the waiting.

All its life. And all for a few delicate moments
in a never-ceasing sky,

which is, of course, only the beginning again.

m.n.risley
August 18, 2012, 3:00 a.m.
